## 2025-05-22 - [Media Selection Feedback]
**Learning:** Providing immediate visual and tactile feedback for selection actions (like long-press to select in a media gallery) significantly improves the perceived responsiveness of the UI. Combining a scale-down animation (0.92f) with haptic feedback creates a "pressed" feel that confirms the action before the user even sees the selection checkmark.
**Action:** Always pair multi-select toggle actions with haptic feedback and a slight scale transition in media-heavy grids.
